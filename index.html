A Mustang® 60 Years in the Making

The Ford Mustang is an iconic American automobile that has been in continuous production since 1964. Known for its sporty design and powerful performance, the Mustang has become a symbol of American muscle cars. Here are some key highlights:

History:

The Mustang was first introduced on April 17, 1964, and quickly became a hit, selling over 400,000 units in its first year1. It is currently in its seventh generation and remains one of Ford’s best-selling models

design:

The Mustang is famous for its “long hood, short deck” proportions, which have influenced the design of many other sporty coupes and convertibles.

Performance :

From the streets to the track, the 2025 Mustang® lineup provides responsive, athletic handling at all speeds. The sport-tuned suspension, selectable drive modes, massive brakes and a flat-bottom steering wheel combine to give you precise control. Top it off with the available GT Performance Package or Mustang Dark Horse™ Handling Package — for a driving experience that lets you feel one with the road.
